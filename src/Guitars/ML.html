Dean ML

The Dean ML is a striking electric guitar known for its bold design and powerful sound, making it a favorite among rock and metal guitarists. Introduced in the 1970s by Dean Guitars, the ML features an aggressive, asymmetrical body shape that not only stands out visually but also enhances playability and comfort. Typically constructed with a solid mahogany body and a set neck, the ML delivers rich, warm tones and impressive sustain. Equipped with high-output humbucking pickups, it excels at producing thick, dynamic sounds suitable for heavy distortion and aggressive playing styles. The ML has been embraced by numerous influential guitarists, including Dimebag Darrell of Pantera, who helped popularize the model in the metal scene. With its distinctive looks, exceptional performance capabilities, and association with legendary musicians, the Dean ML continues to be a sought-after choice for players looking to make a statement on stage.
