Ibanez Iceman

The Iceman is a distinctive electric guitar model produced by Ibanez, known for its unique body shape and versatile sound. Originally introduced in the 1970s, it features a double-cutaway design with a flat top and sharp angles, giving it a modern aesthetic that stands out on stage. The Iceman is favored by rock and metal guitarists for its powerful humbucking pickups, smooth playability, and comfortable neck profile. Notably associated with guitarist Paul Stanley of KISS and later with artists like Joe Satriani and Rob Caggiano, the Iceman offers a wide tonal range, making it suitable for various musical styles, from hard rock to progressive metal. Its combination of striking visuals and exceptional performance capabilities has made it a beloved choice among players.
